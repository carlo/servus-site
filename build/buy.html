Buy Servus, Pick Your Price

This is a "Pay what you want" experiment. Read more…

Here's the thing: pricing software is really, really hard. When I'm trying to find the right price for my applications, my personal formula is this:

(price I would pay for something like it) / 2

Back when I opened my store I knew I would pay ~$20 for a tool like Servus, hence the price should be €8 / $10.

But, frankly, I don't sell as many copies as I'd like. :) Maybe Servus is scratching an itch other people simply don't feel, it's possible. Or maybe I am overvaluing what I've built, also possible.

So I asked some of my customers for their honest opinion. Some told me Servus is too cheap. A few said it's too expensive. Also there are those who think the the price just right. Hooray! Back to square #1.

But the guessing drives me crazy. I'll probably never find the right price on my own; and for the record, the free market fairies are insane. (Selling their apps at €0.79 / $0.99 a pop is not a sound long-term strategy for most developers. Who would've thought?)

So.

Hello, my name is Carlo Zottmann. I am a freelance software developer, 39 years old and married. I'm not starving, but I've invested a lot of time, care and energy in my product, and I'd like to still be able to support it in 1, 2 or 3 years time.

Please think about what Servus is worth to you, then pick a fair price.

Thank you! :)
