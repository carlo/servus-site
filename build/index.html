What it is

It's a menubar icon. Drop a file onto it, and the file will be embedded in a nice preview page, then both are uploaded to your Dropbox. Once done, the link is copied to your clipboard.

Servus comes with a clean built-in preview page theme but you can create your own custom themes, too.

Why it exists

I created Servus because I'm a freelancer myself. Quite often I need to send files to my clients. I feel that embedding those files in a preview page of my own brand makes for a more professional appearance.

Who it is for

Freelancers, agencies, anyone working with clients & customers online, PR people, artists, musicians, street-smart hustlers tryin' to impress their homies with some of dat sweet online bling.

Anyone with a Mac running OS X 10.6.8 or higher on 64 bit (i.e., any Mac built since 2007), and a free or paid Dropbox account.

Servus comes with a clean built-in preview page theme but you can use your own custom themes. (Here's a theme boilerplate kit with documentation to get you started.)

The maximum size of the files you share is only restricted by the size of your Dropbox account. If your account has 1.5GB free space left, then that's your upper file size limit.
